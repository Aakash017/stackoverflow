ABOUT PASARPOLIS

Established in 2015, PasarPolis is Southeast Asia’s largest Insurance Technology (insurtech) platform. PasarPolis’ mission is to redefine insurance so that it can be accessed more easily, quickly, and on target by utilizing the latest technology. PasarPolis’ technology ensures a seamless consumer experience from product selection to claims. After over six years, PasarPolis has become one of the insurtech industry leaders in Indonesia with a focus on affordable insurance products. PasarPolis has also invoked national pride after it started operations in Thailand and Vietnam.
